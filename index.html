featured places

Mount Annapurna, Nepal

Nestled in the heart of north-central Nepal, Mount Annapurna stands majestically at 8,091 meters (26,545 feet), earning its place as the 10th highest peak on Earth.

Musatng, Nepal

Mustang, often referred to as the “Land of Lo,” is a remote and mystical region in north-central Nepal. This once forbidden kingdom provides an unparalleled glimpse into a world where time seems to stand still.

Global Village, Dubai

Global Village Dubai is a vibrant cultural and entertainment destination, showcasing the traditions and cuisines of over 90 countries

Khwaeng wat arun, thailand

This stunning riverside landmark is renowned for its intricate design and colorful ceramic tiles, making it one of Thailand’s most beautiful and historic sites

Angkor Wat, Cambodia

Angkor Wat, Cambodia, is the largest religious monument in the world originally built in the 12th century as a Hindu temple and later transformed into a Buddhist temple. This architectural marvel is renowned for its grandeur, and historical significance

fuvahmulah, maldives

Fuvahmulah, the only single-island atoll in the Maldives, is renowned for its unique biodiversity and stunning landscapes. Located near the equator, this island is a diver’s paradise, famously known as the "Tiger Shark Capital of the World"
